About Us

ISNTCO is an OEM Parts procurement Services Company and always consider your process reliability our #1 priority

ISNTCO is passionate about meeting your energy and industrial challenges with quality, technologically innovative and sustainable solutions and products. As an independent company, we are uniquely positioned to share with you, as committed partners to ensure your engineering ambitions with competitiveness and environmental obligations and meeting your demand of equipment and OEM parts at your door step.

ISNTCO is in the business of procurement and outsourcing services of OEM parts, for the water, power, oil, gas and general industrial plant equipments and parts for the last many years. With our outstanding outsourcing in North America, Europe, Korea and Japan, we can supply your any demand of equipment, accessories and spare parts at a very low cost and in shorter delivery time.
